3.1 Data Cleaning in Item Fat Content.¶

We will start calling the different values from the 'Item Column' to see the inconscistencies from this column.

In [16]:
print(df['Item Fat Content'].unique())

['Regular' 'Low Fat' 'low fat' 'LF' 'reg']
As we can see above, 'low fat' and 'LF' are inconsistent and should be standardized to 'Low Fat'.¶
Similarly, 'reg' should be converted to 'Regular'.¶
In [18]:
df['Item Fat Content']=df['Item Fat Content'].replace({'LF':'Low Fat',
                                 'low fat':'Low Fat',
                                 'reg':'Regular'})

This graph presents total sales by item fat content across the three outlet tiers of the company. 'Low Fat' items lead sales in all three tiers.
